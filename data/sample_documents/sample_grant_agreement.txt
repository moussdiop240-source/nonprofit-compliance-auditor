FEDERAL GRANT AGREEMENT
Grant Number: 2026-HHS-042
Recipient: Community Impact Organization
Award Amount: $125,000
Period: January 1, 2026 – December 31, 2026
Federal Agency: U.S. Department of Health and Human Services

SECTION 1 – PURPOSE
Funds awarded under this agreement support community health outreach programs
in accordance with 2 CFR 200 (Uniform Guidance).

SECTION 2 – ALLOWABLE COSTS
All costs must conform to 2 CFR 200.420. Costs must be necessary, reasonable,
and allocable to the grant program.

SECTION 3 – TRAVEL
Travel costs are allowable per 2 CFR 200.474. Air travel must be economy/coach class.
GSA per diem rates apply for lodging and meals. Mileage reimbursed at IRS standard rate.
Receipts required for any single expense exceeding $75.

SECTION 4 – PERSONNEL
Salary and fringe benefits are allowable when supported by time-and-effort records.
The program coordinator position (100% grant-funded) is pre-approved.

SECTION 5 – EQUIPMENT
Equipment purchases over $5,000 require prior written approval from the grants officer.
Computing devices under $5,000 are allowable as supplies if essential to the project.

SECTION 6 – INDIRECT COSTS
Indirect costs are allowable at the negotiated rate of 15% of direct salaries only.
Overhead allocation must reference the approved indirect cost rate agreement.

SECTION 7 – UNALLOWABLE COSTS
The following are explicitly unallowable under this grant:
- Alcoholic beverages (2 CFR 200.423)
- Lobbying or political activities (2 CFR 200.451)
- Entertainment costs
- Contributions or donations

SECTION 8 – DOCUMENTATION
All expenditures must be supported by original receipts, invoices, or equivalent
documentation retained for three years after the grant closeout date.
