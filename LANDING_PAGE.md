# Nonprofit Compliance Auditor
### AI-Powered Federal Grant Compliance — Instant, Accurate, 100% Private

---

## Stop spending weeks on grant audits. Get answers in minutes.

Federal grant compliance is complex, time-consuming, and unforgiving. One missed regulation can mean returning funds, failing an audit, or losing future grants. The **Nonprofit Compliance Auditor** uses local AI to review every expense against **2 CFR 200 (Uniform Guidance)** — automatically, accurately, and without sending a single byte of your data to the cloud.

---

## Key Benefits

### ⚡ Save Dozens of Hours Per Audit
Upload your expense report and grant agreement. The AI reviews every line item in minutes — not days. Get a full compliance report with executive summary, flagged items, and actionable recommendations instantly.

### ✅ Built on 2 CFR 200 Uniform Guidance
Every decision is grounded in the actual federal regulations. The system cites specific CFR sections (e.g., 2 CFR 200.474 for travel costs), so your auditors know exactly why an item was flagged — and can defend every determination.

### 🔒 100% Private — Runs Locally
Your financial data never leaves your machine. The AI model runs entirely on your hardware via Ollama. No cloud APIs, no third-party servers, no data exposure — just fast, secure compliance analysis.

### 🤖 Smart Pre-Screening
Per-se unallowable items (alcohol, lobbying, entertainment) are caught instantly — before the AI even runs. Statistical anomaly detection flags unusual amounts. Duplicate line items are automatically identified.

### 👁️ Human-in-the-Loop Review
Ambiguous items are automatically routed to your auditor for review. You keep full control — the AI flags, you decide. Every override is recorded for your audit trail.

### 📊 Professional Audit Reports
Export your compliance report as a PDF or Excel workbook — ready to share with grant officers, board members, or external auditors. Includes charts, budget vs. actuals, and a full decision log.
